Project Overview
The goal of this project is to visualize sales data to help a car dealership or manufacturer monitor their Year-to-Date (YTD) performance. The dashboard is split into two primary views:

Overview Panel: High-level summary of sales trends, regional performance, and product distributions.

Details Panel: A granular look at individual transactions for deep-dive auditing.

ðŸ“ˆ Key Metrics (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks three primary KPIs:

YTD Total Sales: $371.19M (representing a 23.59% growth).

YTD Average Price: $28.0K per vehicle.

YTD Total Units Sold: 13K cars (up 24.57% vs previous period).

ðŸ›  Dashboard Features
1. Overview Panel
Sales Weekly Trend: A line chart displaying fluctuations in sales across the 52-week calendar year.

Sales by Body Style & Color: Donut charts illustrating consumer preferences (e.g., SUV, Hatchback, Sedan).

Regional Performance: An interactive map showing sales concentrations in regions like Austin, Janesville, and Scottsdale.

Company Wise Sales Trend: A tabular breakdown of manufacturers (Acura, BMW, Chevrolet, etc.) with integrated sparklines and bar charts for sales share.

2. Details Panel
Transaction Log: A comprehensive table containing Car_id, Date, Customer Name, Dealer Name, and specific vehicle details.

Dynamic Filtering: Sidebar slicers allow users to filter the entire report by:

Body Style

Dealer Name

Transmission Type

Engine Type

Tech Stack
Power BI Desktop: For data modeling and visualization.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used for calculating YTD, MTD, and YoY growth metrics.

Power Query: For data cleaning and transformation (ETL).

Key Insights
Top Manufacturer: Chevrolet leads in sales volume, contributing over 7% of total YTD sales.

Consumer Preference: "Pale White" is a dominant color preference among buyers.

Growth: The significant positive growth in both Total Sales and Units Sold indicates a strong market recovery or successful marketing campaigns during the YTD period.
